Delete your account

App: Prmo (com.aminebassiouni.prmo) · Developer: Amin El Bassiouni

You can delete your Prmo account and all data associated with it at any time, with or without the app installed.

In the app (instant)

  1. Open Prmo and go to Settings (the "You" tab).
  2. Scroll to the Account section.
  3. Tap Delete account and confirm.

Deletion is immediate and permanent.

Without the app (by email)

If you've uninstalled Prmo or can't sign in, email support@prmo.fyi from the email address you used to sign in, with the subject "Delete my account". We verify the request against the sign-in email and complete the deletion within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile (email, @handle, display name).
  • Your daily focus totals, achievements, and best streak.
  • Your friendships, friend requests, group memberships, blocks, and waves.
  • Your push notification token.
  • Your subscription status record (the purchase itself is managed by Google Play / the App Store — cancel it in your store account; deleting the account does not cancel an active subscription).

Tasks and focus sessions stored locally on your device are yours and stay on the device until you delete the app or use "Reset all data" in Settings. No server data is retained after deletion, except records we're legally required to keep (e.g. user reports under content-safety obligations), which are retained for up to 90 days.
