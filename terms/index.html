What Prmo is

Prmo is a focus timer with a social layer. It lets you run focus and lock-in sessions, optionally blocks selected apps via iOS Family Controls, and lets you share daily focus minutes with friends you connect with.

You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete account. This permanently removes your data from our servers (see the Privacy Policy for specifics).

Lock-in mode

Lock-in mode intentionally restricts your ability to pause or stop a focus session, and blocks selected apps for the duration. Once started, the only way to end a Lock-in before its timer expires is to uninstall Prmo, which removes the iOS Managed Settings store and lifts the block. Force-quitting Prmo does not lift the block.

You acknowledge that Lock-in is a deliberate commitment device and that we cannot intervene once a session is running.

User reports and moderation

You can also block any user — they will no longer be able to find you in search, send you friend requests, or see your activity. Blocking is one-directional and silent (the blocked user is not notified).

Friends and visibility

When you accept a friend request, you and that user can see each other's:

  • Display name and handle
  • Today's focus minutes
  • Number of achievements unlocked
  • Best focus streak
  • Current session state (focusing, locked in, or idle)

Your tasks, session notes, and app-blocking selections are never shared.
